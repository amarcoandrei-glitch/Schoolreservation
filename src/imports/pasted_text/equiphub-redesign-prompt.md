### My recommendation for your current screenshots

The biggest improvements would be:

1. **Add equipment images** (your dashboard feels text-heavy).
2. **Replace the gray background with a subtle gradient**.
3. **Use larger KPI cards with charts/sparklines**.
4. **Add a hero section at the top instead of plain "Welcome Back".**
5. **Make the login page split-screen with illustrations.**
6. **Use a collapsible sidebar with active indicators.**
7. **Add dark mode.**
8. **Add charts for equipment usage and reservation trends.**
9. **Use more whitespace and reduce border-heavy boxes.**
10. **Add animations with Framer Motion.**

These changes alone can make it look closer to a professional SaaS product rather than a typical academic system.
